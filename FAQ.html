Frequently asked Questions!

Why do I pay 0.8% fees?

Tipsease is trying very hard to keep the fees for the users as low as possible, but in order to keep Tipsease as awesome as it is, we need to pay our team and server costs.

Is my data secure?

Short answer: Yes!

Longer answer: We are using military grade encryption to make sure your data stays safe. If you would like to know more about this, contact one of ours Devs over the contact form.

I can't find Tipsease on the app store?

Right now Tipsease is not available for all Devices & Countries this is something we are working very hard on to make sure Tipsease is available for everyone soon.

What devices do you support?

Everything from iOS7 & Android Honeycomb upwards is supported by us.

In which countries is Tipsease available?

Right now we are available for download in the US & Europe.

I am missing money in my Tipsease account!

Please contact our help team as soon as possible via the contact form!

I can't delete my Tipsease account.

The reason for this is most likely that you still have money left in your account, please clear you account of all funds and try deleting again. We are sad to see you go, if there is anything we can do better, let us know!

I can't transfer funds out of my account

Please make sure that the bank account in your profile is verified. We only allow cash-out to verified bank accounts for your own security. If someone gets hold of your account he won't be able to take money out of your account.

How do I verify a bank account?

Just put in all your banking information in the required fields, we will then send you a small payment between 0.01$ and 0.20$, to verify your account you need to enter the amount sent in your dashboard.
